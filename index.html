About Me

Hi, I am Caterina Bisiacchi

I am a graduate student at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln and I am currently pursuing my master’s degree in Applied Science, with a major in Leadership Education and a minor in Advertising and Public Relations. Previous to this, I completed my undergraduate studies in Psychological Sciences and Techniques at the University of Trieste in 2023. Following graduation, I decided to embark on a new adventure by moving to Nebraska to pursue my master's degree while also joining the university's swimming and diving team. Looking ahead, I hope to find an internship after graduation to further educate myself in the field of advertising and public relations, thereby contributing to my professional growth. Finally, I intend to return to my home country, Italy, with important knowledge and expertise gained during my time here. While swimming takes up a substantial amount of my time, I also enjoy photography, reading, and sewing. These activities offer a refreshing break from my academic and athletic endeavors.
